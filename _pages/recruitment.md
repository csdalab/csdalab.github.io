# Join the CSDA Lab

Thank you for your interest in the Computational Statistics and Data Analytics (CSDA) Lab.

The CSDA Lab conducts research in trustworthy AI, statistical learning, uncertainty quantification, precision health analytics, and AI-driven decision support systems. Students work on interdisciplinary projects that combine statistics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and domain-specific applications in health, engineering, and society.

---

## Student Opportunities

We welcome motivated undergraduate and graduate students interested in research and professional development in:

* Statistics and data science
* Machine learning and artificial intelligence
* Predictive modeling
* Computational statistics
* Health and biomedical analytics
* AI-driven decision-support systems

Students may participate in:

* Research projects
* Conference presentations
* Journal publications
* Grant-supported research
* Software and application development
* Collaborative projects with external partners

Prior experience in programming (R, Python, or related languages) is helpful but not required. Students with strong quantitative backgrounds and a willingness to learn are encouraged to apply.

---

## Research Assistant Positions

Research assistant opportunities may be available for graduate students and highly motivated undergraduate students.

Preferred backgrounds include:

* Statistics
* Mathematics
* Data Science
* Computer Science
* Engineering
* Related quantitative disciplines

Successful applicants typically demonstrate strong analytical skills, curiosity, professionalism, and an interest in collaborative research.

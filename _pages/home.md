## Computational Statistics and Data Analytics Lab 


### Trustworthy AI, Statistical Learning, and Decision Support

The CSDA Lab develops statistical learning, trustworthy AI, and decision-support methodologies for applications in health, engineering, and society.

Our research integrates statistical modeling, uncertainty quantification, machine learning, and domain expertise to improve prediction, monitoring, and decision making in complex systems.


### Research Programs

- **Trustworthy AI and Statistical Learning**: uncertainty quantification, conformal prediction, robust statistical methods, and interpretable AI.

- **Monitoring and Quality Analytics**: statistical process monitoring, multivariate control charts, IoT monitoring, image analytics, and health-system monitoring.

- **Precision Health Analytics**: sports medicine and orthopedics, cancer survivorship, flow cytometry, clinical and population health analytics, predictive modeling, and causal inference.

- **AI-Driven Decision Support**: educational AI, RAG systems, large language models, intelligent agents, and decision-support applications.
